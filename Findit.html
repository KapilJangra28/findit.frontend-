The Problem

The Campus Dilemma

Navigating a bustling campus means students frequently misplace essential items. Traditional recovery methods are broken.

📋

Paper Notices Fail

Physical bulletin boards are outdated, easily removed, and reach only a fraction of the campus community.

💬

Chat Groups Are Chaotic

Social media groups flood users with spam, expose personal details, and provide zero identity verification.

🔓

No Security Whatsoever

Anyone can claim a found item, creating significant risk of theft and exploitation for vulnerable students.

Core Features

Built for Security & Speed

Three powerful pillars that make Findit the most trusted lost and found platform on campus.

📍
Location Tags

Finders drop a precise digital pin, instantly alerting all students in the exact campus vicinity. Real-time geofencing ensures the right people are notified at the right time.

📸
Photo Uploads

Capture and upload a clear photo of the found item immediately. Our AI-assisted visual matching aids in swift, accurate identification so owners can spot their belongings instantly.

🛡️
Owner Verification

Items are returned safely through hidden questionnaires and secure, private in-app communication channels — ensuring only the true owner can claim their belongings.
